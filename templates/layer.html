Layering is when you wear multiple types of materials to keep you comfortable in multiple weather conditions. A layer can be anything, but they generally fit into 3 categories:
Base layers let you sweat and are comfy to wear.
Mid layers keep you warm when needed
Outer layers protect you (and other layers) from rain, wind, and snow
